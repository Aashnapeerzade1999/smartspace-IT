Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does template customization take? ▼

Standard customization takes 3-5 business days. Complex projects may take up to 2 weeks. We provide a timeline estimate before starting any work.

Do you offer refunds for templates? ▼

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if the template doesn't meet your expectations. Customization services are non-refundable once work has begun.

Can I use your templates for multiple websites? ▼

Our standard license allows use on one website. For multiple websites, you need an extended license. Contact us for pricing.

How does your SEO service work? ▼

We start with a comprehensive website audit, followed by keyword research, on-page optimization, content strategy, and monthly performance reporting.
